About Me

Data scientist, storyteller, and music enthusiast who enjoys solving real-world problems using data.

Music has always been a huge part of my life and one of the main factors that keeps me moving and groovin every day. From 2012 to 2019, I was with SiriusXM, moving up the ranks from Board Engineer to Program Director and working with many of the company’s over 1K stations. Throughout my career in radio I developed a knack for storytelling as a way to promote emerging and established artists, providing them with the tools and a platform to tell their stories. As a Program Director, I began working with data more and more to help develop and launch SiriusXM’s personalization feature, which focused more on the needs of the consumer. It was then when I realized the potential of data to be used as another tool to tell stories. Having completed a data science certification in 2020, I am excited to embrace new challenges in a new era of digital strategy within the entertainment industry by using a combination of interpersonal communication and analytical tools and techniques.
